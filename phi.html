About PHI

PHI is an advanced AI system designed to calculate the golden ratio from any image, starting with human facial analysis.

The golden ratio (approximately 1.618) is a mathematical ratio that appears in nature and has been used in art and architecture for centuries. It's often associated with aesthetic beauty and harmony.

Our AI system can analyze images to detect and measure golden ratio proportions, providing an objective score based on classical indicators of aesthetic balance and symmetry.

The debut application of PHI is in the Miss Banaras competition, where it objectively scores contestants based on facial proportions and symmetry using golden ratio calculations.
