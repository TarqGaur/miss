Eligibility & Criteria

The Miss Banaras Competition celebrates the grace, talent, and cultural richness of Banaras through its women. This prestigious event is designed to showcase not just beauty, but intellect, cultural awareness, and social responsibility. Review our eligibility requirements and judging criteria below.

Basic Requirements

  • Age between 14 to 30 years as of competition date Required
  • Participants under 18 must have parental/guardian approval Required
  • Must be a resident of Varanasi OR currently working/studying in Varanasi
  • Must be able to communicate in Hindi (English is a plus but not mandatory)
  • No prior professional modeling contract requirement

Additional Requirements

  • Participants should stay actively involved throughout the competition.
  • Should have knowledge about Varanasi's culture and heritage
  • Must have a valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, School/College ID, etc.)
  • Should be willing to participate in community service activities
  • No criminal record or ongoing legal proceedings

Judging Criteria & Weightage

Our judging process combines cutting-edge AI assessment with human expertise and public opinion to ensure a fair and comprehensive evaluation of each participant.

80%

AI Assessment

Our proprietary AI model evaluates participants based on their images and answers, analyzing factors like presentation, confidence, cultural awareness, and communication skills.

15%

Expert Panel

A panel of respected judges from fashion, arts, culture, and social work will evaluate participants on their talent, presence, and potential.

5%

Public Vote

Community engagement and popularity will contribute to the final score, enabling the public to have a voice in the selection process.

What qualities are assessed?

  • Cultural knowledge and appreciation of Banaras heritage
  • Communication skills and articulation
  • Confidence and stage presence
  • Social awareness and community involvement
  • Talent showcase (performing arts, creative skills)
  • Poise and presentation
  • Leadership qualities and future vision

Competition Process

Phase 1

Registration & Initial Screening

Submit your application online with required photos and answers to screening questions. Our AI will conduct initial assessment.

Phase 2

Quarter Finals

Selected candidates participate in virtual or in-person cultural knowledge assessment and personality interviews.

Phase 3

Semi Finals

Showcase your talent, participate in group discussions on social issues, and demonstrate your knowledge of Banaras culture.

Phase 4

Public Voting

Profiles of semi-finalists will be available for public voting that contributes 5% to final scores.

Final Event

Grand Finale

A spectacular celebration of Banaras culture where finalists showcase their journey, followed by the announcement of results and prize distribution.
